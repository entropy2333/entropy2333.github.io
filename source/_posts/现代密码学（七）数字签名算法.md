## RSA

$$
h = H(M) \\
S = h^d\bmod N \\
S^e = (h^e)^d = h\bmod N
$$

## El Gamal

选择随机数k，满足$gck(k,\ p-1)=1$，计算密钥
$$
K = a^k\bmod p
$$

用Euclidean扩展算法求解S
$$
M = x\cdot K + k\cdot S\bmod(p-1)
$$
也即
$$
S = k^{-1}(M-x\cdot K)\bmod(p-1)
$$
签名就是$(M,K,S)$$
$$
y^K\cdot K^S = (a^x)^K\cdot (a^k)^{k^{-1}(M-x\cdot K)} = a^M \bmod p
$$
